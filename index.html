Neighbour

Neighbours was the final group project I completed during my bootcamp. The app's main objective is to foster a strong sense of community by connecting residents and facilitating interactions within our local area. As the tech lead, I provided continuous support to my teams throughout the entire full-stack workflow, which led to the successful development of this social app within a tight 7-day timeframe. Our hard work paid off, and we had the exciting opportunity to present Neighbours at Dev Academy's showcase night.

FurEverFriend

FurEverFriend is a full stack app I developed independently after completing the BootCamp. Using the knowledge gained during the program, I planned and built the app. It serves as a platform to connect dog owners, allowing their dogs to find compatible friends and enabling owners to connect with like-minded individuals. It's a project that combines my technical skills and passion for fostering connections within the dog owner community.
